Discrete Fourier Transform

This JavaScript project allows you to draw a small image by hand, and have it approximated and recreated using the Fourier Transform. Just click in the small sketch window and draw your image. When you're happy with it, click again within the window to stop drawing, and then click the "Generate Sketch" button and watch it get recreated!
